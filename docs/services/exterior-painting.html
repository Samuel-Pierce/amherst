When Quality Matters

Exterior Painting Services

General Painting & Staining

From simple jobs to complex projects, our experienced staff of professional painters can quickly and efficiently complete any interior projects you have in mind.

Carpentry

Some projects call for more than just a new coat of paint, especially outside where your home endures the most wear and tear. Our full-time carpenter has over a decade of experience installing and replacing trim, clapboard/siding, decking, windows, sills, doors, thresholds and more.

Deck Restorations

Decks, spindles, railings and stairs take a beating from you, the sun and the harsh New England weather. Let us do the laborious work of restoring your deck to new.

Pressure / Power Washing

Think the outside of your home is clean? Think again. Mold, mildew, dirt pollen and more cover every nook and cranny of your home. A chemical treatment followed by a pressure wash is the only way to get your home clean, and sometimes it's all you need.

Color Conultations

Can't make up your mind? Not a problem! Let our personal color consultant guide you with a color consultation. Color consultations are free for all contracted work.

Trade Referals

Are you new to the area and looking for a plumber, roofer, or electrician? We work with all of the trades every day. Ask us for a referral!
